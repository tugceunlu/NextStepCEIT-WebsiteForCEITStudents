CEIT421 Research Methods

This introductory course provides prospective teachers with a structured framework to understand research methodologies in educational settings. Through a series of readings, class discussions, and assignments, students will explore key aspects of social science research, including problem identification, literature review, research design, data collection, analysis, and reporting. The course emphasizes practical applications, focusing on how classroom research can be effectively implemented and evaluated
