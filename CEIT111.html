CEIT111 Information Technology in Education I

Computer introduction and the use of information systems in education. Data display and computer evolution. Computer system components include microcomputers, operating systems and environments, Windows, input-output devices, and supplemental storage devices. Text management: processors. Spreadsheets are used to manage numbers. effects of computers on security, ethics, and society


    Course Objectives

  • The terminology and concepts of computing
  • The role of computers in society, organizations and education
  • Using word-processing, spreadsheet and presentation software
