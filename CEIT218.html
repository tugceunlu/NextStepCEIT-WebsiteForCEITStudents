CEIT218 - Graphics and Animation in Education

This course introduces graphics and animation as communication tools in education. It covers graphic design principles, 2D and 3D design, typography, logo design, and animation techniques. Students will gain hands-on experience with Photoshop, Blender, and other design tools, applying their knowledge to real-world educational content.


    Course Objectives

  • Understand graphic design principles (balance, contrast, alignment, proximity, repetition).
  • Work with typography, colors, signs, logos, and trademarks.
  • Learn about image compression, resolution, layers, filters, and effects.
  • Develop 2D graphics for instructional and awareness posters.
  • Create 3D models and animations using Blender.
  • Apply animation techniques in educational settings.
