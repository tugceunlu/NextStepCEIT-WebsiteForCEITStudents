CEIT112 Information Technology in Education II

This course provides a comprehensive knowledge into database management and Microsoft Access, designed to equip students with the skills to organize, analyze, and manage data efficiently. Students will delve into foundational concepts, intermediate techniques, and advanced applications in a progressive manner, ensuring both a solid understanding and practical proficiency.


    Course Objectives

  • The fundamental concepts of database management systems and the principles of relational database design
  • How to effectively use Microsoft Access 2021, including navigating its interface and utilizing its core features
  • Techniques for creating and managing tables, relationships, and queries to organize and analyze data
  • The process of designing and generating customized forms and reports to meet specific user requirements
  • Methods to ensure data security and ethical practices in data management and ownership
