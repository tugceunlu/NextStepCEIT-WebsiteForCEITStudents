CEIT407 & CEIT408 Teaching Practicum

This course provides prospective teachers with hands-on experience in real classroom settings. Students will engage in teaching practice under supervision, observe effective instructional strategies, and develop their own lesson plans. This practicum is designed to enhance teaching competence, classroom management skills, and the ability to integrate technology into education.


    Course Objectives

  • Classroom observation and analysis of teaching methods.
  • Planning, developing, and executing lesson plans in real classroom environments.
  • Effective classroom management and student engagement techniques.
  • Use of technology and instructional materials in teaching.
  • Demonstrate effective classroom management skills.
  • Reflect on their teaching practice to identify strengths and areas for improvement.
