CEIT435 & CEIT436 Project Development and Management

CEIT435 and CEIT436 focus on project development and management in educational technology and instructional design. These courses guide students through the theory and practice of project management, covering proposal writing, planning, implementation, evaluation, and reporting.In CEIT435, students develop project proposals, conduct needs analysis, and create a structured project plan. In CEIT436, students implement, monitor progress, and finalize their projects, culminating in a final project report and presentation.

    Course Objectives

  • Understand project management principles in educational and technological contexts.
  • Develop structured project proposals, including problem statements and objectives.
  • Create work breakdown structures (WBS) and Gantt charts for project planning.
  • Implement project tracking and monitoring techniques.
  • Utilize project management tools for cost estimation and resource allocation.
  • Conduct project evaluations and compile comprehensive reports.
  • Work effectively in teams to execute and present projects.
