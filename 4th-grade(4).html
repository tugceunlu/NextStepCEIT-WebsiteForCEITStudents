4th Year Courses

CEIT407 & CEIT408 Teaching Practicum

This course provides prospective teachers with hands-on experience in real classroom settings. Students will engage in teaching practice under supervision, observe effective instructional strategies, and develop their own lesson plans. This practicum is designed to enhance teaching competence, classroom management skills, and the ability to integrate technology into education.

Learn More

CEIT419 Web Design

CEIT419 focuses on fundamentals of web design, covering HTML5, CSS3, and responsive web design techniques. Students will build and style web pages, work with layouts, and create interactive elements. The course emphasizes modern design principles, prototyping, and user experience considerations using Visual Studio Code (VS Code) as the primary development environment.

Learn More

CEIT421 Research Methods

This introductory course provides prospective teachers with a structured framework to understand research methodologies in educational settings. Through a series of readings, class discussions, and assignments, students will explore key aspects of social science research, including problem identification, literature review, research design, data collection, analysis, and reporting. The course emphasizes practical applications, focusing on how classroom research can be effectively implemented and evaluated

Learn More

CEIT435 & CEIT436 Project Development and Management

CEIT435 and CEIT436 focus on project development and management in educational technology and instructional design. These courses guide students through the theory and practice of project management, covering proposal writing, planning, implementation, evaluation, and reporting.
