CEIT386 Community Service

CEIT386 focuses on fostering awareness about community service and social responsibility in education. Students will identify societal issues, develop service projects, and volunteer with non-profit organizations. The course also emphasizes organizing and participating in public discussions, such as conferences, panels, and symposia, while gaining practical experience in community service initiatives.


    Course Objectives

  • Recognize and analyze social problems in education.
  • Develop strategies and solutions for addressing educational challenges.
  • Engage in voluntary service activities with non-profit organizations.
  • Participate in community-based events such as discussions, workshops, and conferences.
  • Reflect on and share experiences from community service projects.

Organizations

  • TEGV (Türkiye Eğitim Gönüllüleri Vakfı) – A foundation supporting education for children.
  • LÖSEV (Lösemili Çocuklar Vakfı) – An organization providing support for children with leukemia.
  • İLKYAR (İlköğretim Okullarına Yardım Vakfı) – Supports students in rural primary schools.
  • TZV (Türk Zekâ Vakfı) – Works on gifted education and intelligence development.
  • ODTÜ Toplum ve Bilim Uygulama ve Araştırma Merkezi – Conducts social and scientific community projects.
  • Yeşilay – A foundation focused on addiction prevention and social health.
