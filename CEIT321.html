CEIT321 Foundations of Distance Education

CEIT321 introduces students to theoretical frameworks, historical development, and practical applications of distance education. The course covers synchronous and asynchronous learning, instructional design for online education, and emerging trends in distance learning technologies. Students will explore various distance learning models, research methodologies, and evaluation techniques for online learning environments.


    Course Objectives

  • Understand the history, purpose, and evolution of distance education.
  • Define key concepts, terminology, and theoretical models in distance education.
  • Analyze technologies used in distance education, including online platforms and communication tools.
  • Compare synchronous and asynchronous learning strategies.
  • Evaluate instructional design models for distance education.
  • Assess the effectiveness of distance learning programs and strategies.
  • Identify trends and challenges in modern distance education.
